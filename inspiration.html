My interests in IT are diverse and date back many years to the early 90s, when our family received our first PC, I was a somewhat homely child so after playing all the awesome games there was to offer, Commander Keen, DOOM, Wolfenstein3D, I started getting interested in just how these marvels of technology worked, inside and out. It was then by chance discovered a wonderful operating system called ‘LINUX’ and something equally as wonderful called ‘open-source software’ and I guess it has just spiraled from there.
